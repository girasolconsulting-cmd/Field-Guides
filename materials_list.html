Notes on costs: The microscope (~$950) is the dominant cost. The AmScope SM-4TZ-144A-18M3 with built-in 18MP USB camera is recommended as an all-in-one unit — it avoids the need to purchase a separate camera adapter. If budget is constrained, a binocular scope with a separately purchased USB camera adapter (~$150–200) is a lower-cost alternative, though less convenient. All consumables (ethanol, saline, gloves) should be sourced locally in South Africa to avoid import and shipping costs. USD prices do not include shipping or import duties to South Africa.
