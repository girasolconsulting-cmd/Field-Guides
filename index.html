Bee Research Field Guides

South Africa — Apis mellifera subspecies — Honey & Colony Health

A collection of field and laboratory guides for bee identification, honey sampling, and colony health assessment — focused on the two South African honey bee subspecies A. m. capensis and A. m. scutellata. All guides are designed to be used in the field and in the lab.
